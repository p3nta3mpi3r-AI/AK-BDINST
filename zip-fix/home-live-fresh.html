Why Donate Blood in Oklahoma?

Your donation makes a real difference in your community while providing benefits for you.

❤️

Save Local Lives

Your donation helps trauma patients, cancer patients, and children in Oklahoma hospitals. Every donation can save up to three lives.

💵

Earn Compensation

Receive modest compensation, gift cards, or rewards for your time and donation. Policies vary by location and donation type.

🩹

Safe & Professional

All donations are performed by trained medical professionals using sterile, single-use equipment in a safe, monitored environment.
