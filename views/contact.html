Common Questions

How do I schedule a donation?

Visit our online scheduling form or call any donor center directly. Walk-ins are also welcome at most locations.

Am I eligible to donate?

Most healthy adults 16+ (with parental consent) or 17+ who weigh at least 110 lbs can donate. Check full eligibility requirements.

Want to host a blood drive?

We partner with businesses, schools, churches, and community organizations. Call (877) 340-8777 to discuss hosting a drive.

Need more information?

Browse our FAQ page for detailed answers, or visit How It Works for a step-by-step overview.
