About Oklahoma Blood Donors

Connecting Oklahoma communities with life-saving blood donation opportunities while supporting donors.

Our Mission

Oklahoma Blood Donors is dedicated to connecting generous Oklahomans with Our Blood Institute donor centers across the state. We believe that saving lives should be accessible, rewarding, and community-centered.

Every day, patients in Oklahoma hospitals need blood for surgeries, cancer treatment, trauma care, and chronic illness management. Your donation directly impacts your neighbors, friends, and community members in their time of greatest need.

❤️

Life-Saving Impact

Every blood donation can save up to three lives. We help facilitate thousands of donations each year, directly supporting Oklahoma hospitals and patients.

🛡

Safety First

All Our Blood Institute centers maintain the highest safety standards with trained medical professionals and sterile, single-use equipment.

🏠

Community-Centered

We serve communities across Oklahoma, from Oklahoma City and Tulsa to smaller towns like Ada, Ardmore, and Enid.

🌟

Donor Support

We recognize the value of your time and commitment through compensation programs and rewards for donors.

Our Partnership with Our Blood Institute

We work closely with Our Blood Institute (OBI), Oklahoma's community blood center, to connect donors with their 10+ donor centers across the state.

About Our Blood Institute

Our Blood Institute is a nonprofit community blood center that has been serving Oklahoma for decades. They collect, test, and distribute blood products to hospitals throughout the state, ensuring a safe and adequate blood supply for patients in need.

OBI operates donor centers in major cities and smaller communities across Oklahoma, making it convenient for donors to give blood close to home. Their professional medical staff ensures every donation is safe, comfortable, and makes a real difference.

Our Values

Transparency

We provide honest, accurate information about the donation process, compensation, and requirements. We never make exaggerated claims or promises.

Safety

Donor safety is paramount. We emphasize that blood donation is a medical procedure that requires honest health disclosure and professional medical oversight.

Community Focus

We believe in the power of local communities helping local patients. Your donation stays in Oklahoma, helping your neighbors in their time of need.

Donor Recognition

We believe donors should be recognized for their time and commitment. Compensation and rewards help acknowledge the valuable contribution donors make to saving lives.
