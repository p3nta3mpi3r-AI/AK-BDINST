About Oklahoma Blood Donors

Connecting Oklahoma communities with life-saving blood donation opportunities since 2026.

What is Oklahoma Blood Donors?

Oklahoma Blood Donors is a digital platform that simplifies the path from intention to donation. We connect Oklahoma residents with certified blood and plasma donor centers across the state, providing clear information about eligibility, locations, compensation, and scheduling — all in one place.

Every day, patients in Oklahoma hospitals need blood for surgeries, cancer treatment, trauma care, and chronic illness management. Oklahoma’s blood supply depends on voluntary donors, yet many potential donors don’t know where to go, what to expect, or whether they’re eligible. We exist to close that gap.

Meet the Founder

PI

Pierre Isidor

Founder, Oklahoma Blood Donors

Pierre Isidor founded Oklahoma Blood Donors after noticing a critical gap in how Oklahomans access blood donation information. Potential donors often wanted to help but couldn’t easily find accurate, consolidated information about where to donate, what to expect, or how to schedule an appointment.

Working at the intersection of public health and technology, Pierre built oklahomabloodinstitute.com to be the most complete blood donation resource for the state of Oklahoma — covering every major city and over 120 ZIP codes with localized donor center information, eligibility guides, and scheduling tools.

The platform serves thousands of Oklahoma donors and partners closely with certified donor centers to ensure all information is accurate, current, and aligned with FDA blood donation regulations.

How We Work with Oklahoma Donor Centers

Oklahoma Blood Donors is an independent information and scheduling platform. We aggregate public information about certified donor centers across Oklahoma, including addresses, hours, phone numbers, and donation types available.

Our scheduling links connect you directly with donor centers through their own appointment systems. We do not collect blood, process donations, or make medical eligibility decisions — those are handled by trained medical professionals at each center.

Oklahoma’s blood supply is managed primarily by Our Blood Institute (OBI), a nonprofit community blood center operating 10+ donor centers statewide. We help connect donors to OBI and other certified facilities.

Our Values

Transparency

Honest, accurate information about the donation process, compensation, and requirements. We never make exaggerated claims or promises about earnings or health benefits.

Safety

Donor safety is paramount. Blood donation is a medical procedure requiring honest health disclosure and professional oversight. We emphasize this on every page.

Community Focus

Your donation stays in Oklahoma, helping patients at local hospitals. We serve communities from Oklahoma City and Tulsa to Ada, Ardmore, Bartlesville, and everywhere in between.

Accessibility

Blood donation information should be easy to find for every Oklahoman. That’s why we built ZIP-code-level pages, city guides, and detailed eligibility content — so you can find exactly what you need.
