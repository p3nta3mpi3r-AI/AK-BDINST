Why OU Students Donate

Blood donation has long been a part of OU campus culture. From Greek life philanthropy events to athletics-affiliated drives, the Sooner community consistently shows up to support Our Blood Institute's mission of keeping Oklahoma's blood supply stable.

Campus Culture & Greek Life

OU Greek chapters regularly organize blood drives as part of their community service commitments. These drives are open to all OU students and community members, not just chapter members. Pre-health student organizations also participate, using blood donation as a way to accumulate community service hours and gain exposure to medical settings.

Athletics Drives

OU athletics-affiliated drives typically coincide with football season and other major sporting events, drawing large numbers of donors from the broader Norman community. These are excellent opportunities to donate while connecting with fellow Sooner fans.

How to Sign Up

Use the Donable platform to schedule your appointment at an upcoming OU campus blood drive or at the Norman Blood Donor Center. Walk-ins are accepted at the Norman center during regular hours. For campus drives, an appointment ensures your spot and reduces wait times.

What to Bring as a Student Donor

✓ Bring These

  • Valid photo ID (OU student ID, driver's license, or passport)
  • List of any current medications
  • A full stomach — eat a good meal beforehand
  • Water — stay well hydrated

Good to Know

  • 17+ donors do NOT need parental consent
  • Wear a shirt with sleeves that roll up easily
  • Plan for about 45 minutes total
  • Snacks are provided after donation

OU Blood Drive FAQs

Where can OU students donate blood near campus?
OU students can donate at on-campus drives organized through Our Blood Institute, or visit the Norman Blood Donor Center at 1004 24th Avenue NW — just minutes from campus. The Norman center accepts walk-ins during regular hours.
What are the Norman Blood Donor Center hours?
Mon: 7:30 AM–6 PM | Tue: 10 AM–6 PM | Wed: 7:30 AM–4:30 PM | Thu: 10 AM–6 PM | Fri: 7:30 AM–4:30 PM | Sat: 7:30 AM–2 PM | Sun: Closed. Call (405) 928-6500 to confirm hours before visiting.
Do OU students need parental consent to donate?
No parental consent is required for donors aged 17 and older. Students who are 17 can donate on their own with a valid photo ID.
Does OU have Greek life blood drives?
Yes. OU Greek chapters regularly partner with Our Blood Institute to host on-campus blood drives open to all OU students and community members who meet eligibility requirements.
How do I schedule a blood donation at OU?
Use the Donable scheduling platform to book your appointment at an upcoming OU campus blood drive or at the Norman Blood Donor Center. You can also call the Norman center at (405) 928-6500.
