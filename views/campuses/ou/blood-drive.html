Two Ways to Donate Near OU

On-Campus Blood Drive

OBI hosts drives on the OU campus multiple times per semester through Greek chapters, student organizations, and athletics partnerships.

  • Convenient — on campus between classes
  • Walk-ins usually welcome
  • Seasonal schedule — check Donable

Norman Blood Donor Center

Open 6 days a week. No campus drive needed — walk in or schedule any time during regular hours.

  • 1004 24th Avenue NW, Norman OK
  • Phone: (405) 928-6500
  • Open Mon–Sat (see hours below)
