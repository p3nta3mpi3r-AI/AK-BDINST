Campus Blood Drive FAQs

Which Oklahoma colleges host blood drives?
Oklahoma State University in Stillwater and the University of Oklahoma in Norman are the two primary universities hosting regular drives through Our Blood Institute. Drives occur multiple times per semester.
How do I sign up for a campus blood drive?
Use the Donable scheduling link to reserve your spot at an upcoming campus drive. Walk-ins are welcome at most locations, but appointments are encouraged to guarantee your preferred time slot.
Can college students donate blood?
Yes. Students 17 and older can donate without parental consent. You must weigh at least 110 pounds and be in generally good health. Bring a valid photo ID such as a student ID or driver's license.
How often do campus blood drives happen?
Campus blood drives typically run every 4 to 8 weeks throughout the fall and spring semesters. OSU and OU both host multiple drives per semester across various campus locations including Greek chapter houses, recreation centers, and student unions.
