What to Expect at the Tulsa Center

The Tulsa Blood Donor Center provides a comfortable, professional environment for blood and plasma donations. Our trained staff will guide you through the entire process, from registration to recovery.

Eligibility Requirements

  • At least 17 years old (16 with parental consent)
  • Weigh at least 110 pounds
  • Be in generally good health
  • Valid photo ID required
