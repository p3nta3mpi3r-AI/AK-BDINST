What to Bring

Make your donation appointment as smooth as possible by coming prepared. Here's what every donor needs:

  • Photo ID

    Driver's license, state ID, passport, or other government-issued photo identification.

  • Comfortable clothing

    Wear a short-sleeved shirt or clothing with sleeves that roll up easily above the elbow.

  • Eat a meal 2 hours before

    A light, iron-rich meal before donating helps maintain blood sugar and improves your experience.

  • Drink water

    Stay well-hydrated before and after your donation — aim for at least 16 oz of water in the hours leading up to your appointment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about scheduling blood donations in Oklahoma.

Scheduling a blood donation appointment takes about 2 minutes online through Donable. Create your free account, select a location, and pick a time slot — that's it. You'll receive a confirmation immediately.

Yes, walk-ins are welcome at all Our Blood Institute locations across Oklahoma. However, scheduling an appointment in advance guarantees your slot and typically reduces your wait time — especially during busy periods.

Most Our Blood Institute donor centers are open Monday through Sunday. Weekday hours are typically 7:00 AM – 7:00 PM, and weekend hours are generally 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM. Check your preferred location page for exact hours.

Same-day appointments are often available. You can schedule as little as a few hours before you plan to donate, though booking a day or two ahead gives you the best selection of time slots — particularly at busy metro-area centers.

Compensation varies by donation type (whole blood, plasma, platelets) and by location. Some donation types and centers offer financial compensation or rewards for your time. Contact your nearest Our Blood Institute center directly or review current offers on the Donable platform for the most up-to-date information.
