Donating Blood in the Midwest City Area

Oklahoma Blood Institute maintains donation centers throughout Midwest City to serve donors in ZIP code 73110 and surrounding communities. Your donation directly supports patients at Oklahoma hospitals who need blood for surgery, cancer treatment, and trauma care.

To donate, you must be at least 17 years old, weigh at least 110 pounds, be in good health, and bring a valid photo ID. No appointment is required, but scheduling ahead ensures minimal wait times.
