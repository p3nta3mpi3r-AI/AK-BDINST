Ardmore occupies a strategic position in southern Oklahoma, sitting near the Red River and serving as the primary population center for Carter, Garvin, and Murray counties. The Ardmore Blood Donor Center at 2235 Merrick Drive draws from a donor base that includes Lone Grove, Dickson, Springer, and Wilson, as well as patients who commute from smaller surrounding communities. Blood donated in Ardmore supports Mercy Hospital Ardmore, the largest hospital in southern Oklahoma.

How do I donate blood in Ardmore, Oklahoma?

Southern Oklahoma has a significant emergency and trauma medicine need driven by I-35 corridor traffic, agricultural accidents, and the region's relatively limited hospital network. Mercy Hospital Ardmore is the primary trauma facility for a large geographic area — which means its blood supply requirements are high relative to the local donor population. Donors who give consistently in Ardmore are providing an outsized contribution compared to donors in more urban markets.

What should I bring to donate blood in Ardmore?

Ardmore's proximity to the Texas border creates a unique donor dynamic — some residents commute across state lines for work, which can make weekday donation windows tight. Weekend and early-morning appointment slots fill quickly at the Ardmore center; booking online in advance is the most reliable way to secure your preferred time. Donors who can commit to a regular every-other-month schedule are especially valuable to this regional center.
