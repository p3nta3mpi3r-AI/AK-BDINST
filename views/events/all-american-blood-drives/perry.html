Blood Donation in Perry, Oklahoma (73077)

As part of Oklahoma’s heartland, Perry (73077) has a proud tradition of community giving. The All American Blood Drives are Our Blood Institute’s largest annual summer campaign, bringing blood donation directly into communities with 1 blood drive in the Perry area.

Oklahoma’s hospitals need 800+ units of blood every day. Perry area donors play a vital role in keeping this supply steady. Every two seconds, someone in the U.S. needs a blood transfusion. Summer is the toughest season for blood banks — regular donors travel, school drives stop, and supply dips just as demand holds steady.

What You Get for Donating in Perry

Every successful blood donor at an All American Blood Drive receives three free gifts (while supplies last):

  • Limited-edition “All American Blood Donor” t-shirt — exclusive patriotic design
  • Folding camp chair — perfect for summer cookouts and fireworks
  • Theme park voucher — free admission to a participating local attraction

How Blood Donation Works

  1. Arrive at the Perry drive location — walk-ins welcome, appointments optional
  2. Mini-physical — quick pulse, blood pressure, temperature, hemoglobin check (~5 min)
  3. Donate — the actual blood draw takes just 8–10 minutes
  4. Recover & celebrate — enjoy snacks and pick up your free gifts

One Donation = Three Lives

Your single pint of whole blood is separated into red blood cells (for trauma and surgery), platelets (for cancer patients), and plasma (for burn victims and bleeding disorders). One donation in Perry can directly help three patients in Oklahoma hospitals.

Eligibility

  • At least 16 years old (parental consent for age 16)
  • Weigh at least 110 pounds
  • In general good health and feeling well
  • No whole blood donation in past 56 days
  • Valid photo ID required

Questions? Call Our Blood Institute at 877-340-8777 or check full eligibility requirements.

Tips for Perry Donors

  • Eat an iron-rich meal 2–3 hours before (spinach, beans, lean meat)
  • Drink 16 oz of extra water the morning of your donation
  • Avoid fatty foods before donating
  • Get a full night’s sleep
  • Wear a shirt with sleeves that roll up easily
