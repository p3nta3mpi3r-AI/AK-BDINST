Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

OBI Blood Donor ("we," "our," or "us") operates this website to connect blood donors with Our Blood Institute donor centers across Oklahoma. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our website or submit a scheduling form:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Preferred donor center and appointment date
  • ZIP code or location information
  • Website usage data (cookies, analytics)

How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate donation appointment scheduling
  • To connect you with your preferred donor center
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send appointment reminders and updates (with your consent)

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with Our Blood Institute donor centers to facilitate your donation appointment. We may also use third-party analytics services to understand website usage patterns.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact your nearest donor center or reach out through our website to exercise these rights.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use this website. GA4 collects anonymized data about page views, session duration, and general interaction patterns. This data is processed by Google and governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies

We use cookies for website functionality (like remembering your theme preference) and analytics (Google Analytics 4). You can disable cookies in your browser settings, which will also prevent analytics tracking.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact your nearest OBI Blood Donor donor center. Visit our Locations page for contact information.
