Red Cross in Oklahoma

The American Red Cross does operate in Oklahoma. Its primary blood donation centers in the state are located in Oklahoma City (701 N. Lindsay) and Tulsa (10151 E. 11th St.). You can reach the Red Cross donation line at 1-800-RED-CROSS or schedule online at RedCrossBlood.org.

The Red Cross is a vital national organization that responds to disasters and supports military families in addition to collecting blood. However, blood collected by the Red Cross may be redistributed nationally — it does not stay exclusively in Oklahoma.

Which Should Oklahoma Donors Choose?

If your goal is to keep your blood donation in Oklahoma and directly support Oklahoma patients, OBI is the preferred choice. With 10+ centers statewide, Donation Process programs, and an exclusive focus on Oklahoma hospitals, OBI makes it easy for Oklahomans to donate locally and know their contribution stays home.

The Red Cross is a fine option if you are traveling or live closer to a Red Cross center, and their contribution to disaster relief and national blood supply is significant. Both organizations do important work.

For most Oklahoma residents, OBI's local focus, broader in-state center network, and Donation Process programs make it the practical first choice.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Our Blood Institute and the American Red Cross?

Our Blood Institute (OBI) is Oklahoma's local, independent blood bank — the 6th largest in the United States. It focuses exclusively on serving Oklahoma hospitals and patients. The American Red Cross is a national organization operating across the country. For Oklahoma donors who want their blood to stay in Oklahoma, OBI is the local choice.

Does the Red Cross operate in Oklahoma?

Yes. The American Red Cross has donor centers in Oklahoma, including locations in Oklahoma City (701 N. Lindsay) and Tulsa (10151 E. 11th St.). However, blood collected by the Red Cross may be distributed nationally. You can reach Red Cross at 1-800-RED-CROSS.

Does Our Blood Institute offer Donation Process for donations?

Yes. OBI offers Donation Process and rewards programs for eligible donors, particularly for plasma and certain other donation types. The Red Cross does not compensate whole blood donors. Check with your nearest OBI center for current Donation Process details.

Does donating at OBI keep my blood in Oklahoma?

Yes. Our Blood Institute serves Oklahoma hospitals exclusively. Blood donated at OBI centers stays in Oklahoma and goes directly to Oklahoma patients. The Red Cross distributes blood nationally and cannot guarantee your donation stays local.

How many OBI donor centers are there in Oklahoma?

Our Blood Institute operates 10+ donor centers across Oklahoma, including locations in Oklahoma City, Tulsa, Edmond, Norman, Lawton, and other communities. Use the locations page to find the center nearest you.
