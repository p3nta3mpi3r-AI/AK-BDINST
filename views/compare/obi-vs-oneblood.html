Our Blood Institute vs. OneBlood — Oklahoma Donors Guide

OneBlood primarily serves Florida and the Southeast United States. Our Blood Institute (OBI) is Oklahoma's dedicated local blood bank serving Oklahoma, Arkansas, and Texas. For Oklahoma donors, OBI is the correct choice — OneBlood does not operate centers in Oklahoma.

The Short Answer for Oklahoma Donors

If you live in Oklahoma and want to donate blood, Our Blood Institute is your organization. OneBlood does not have centers in Oklahoma — it is a Florida-based blood bank. Searching for "OneBlood Oklahoma" will not produce relevant local results. OBI has 10+ centers across Oklahoma and is the state's dedicated blood supply provider.

Our Blood Institute (OBI)

OBI is Oklahoma's local, independent blood bank — the 6th largest in the United States. Founded to serve Oklahoma's healthcare system, OBI operates 10+ donor centers statewide and ensures 100% of donations serve Oklahoma hospitals and patients.

  • ● Headquartered in Oklahoma City
  • ● Serves Oklahoma, Arkansas, and Texas
  • ● 10+ Oklahoma donor centers
  • ● Compensation programs available
  • ● The right choice for Oklahoma donors

OneBlood

OneBlood is a large nonprofit blood center based in Florida. It serves hospitals throughout Florida and parts of the southeastern United States — including Georgia, Alabama, North Carolina, and South Carolina — but does not operate in Oklahoma.

  • ● Headquartered in Orlando, Florida
  • ● Serves Florida and the Southeast
  • ● No centers in Oklahoma
  • ● Highly regarded in its service area
  • ● Not the right option for Oklahoma donors

Did You Recently Move to Oklahoma?

If you previously donated at a OneBlood center in Florida or another southeastern state and have recently moved to Oklahoma, your next donation should be at an OBI center. OBI has 10+ locations across the state, and your past donation history through OneBlood does not transfer — you'll register fresh at an OBI center.

The process is straightforward: bring a valid photo ID, be prepared to answer a brief health questionnaire, and enjoy the local connection of knowing your blood supports Oklahoma patients. Find your nearest OBI center here.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does OneBlood operate in Oklahoma?

No. OneBlood primarily serves Florida and the southeastern United States. OneBlood does not operate donor centers in Oklahoma. Oklahoma residents should donate through Our Blood Institute (OBI), which is Oklahoma's dedicated local blood bank with 10+ centers statewide.

What is the difference between Our Blood Institute and OneBlood?

Our Blood Institute (OBI) is Oklahoma's local independent blood bank serving Oklahoma, Arkansas, and Texas. OneBlood is a large regional blood bank based in Florida, serving primarily Florida and nearby southeastern states. The two organizations serve entirely different geographic areas — OBI is the correct choice for Oklahoma donors.

Where should I donate blood if I live in Oklahoma?

Oklahoma residents should donate at Our Blood Institute (OBI), which is the state's dedicated local blood bank. OBI has 10+ donor centers across Oklahoma including Oklahoma City, Tulsa, Edmond, Norman, Lawton, and more. Your donation stays in Oklahoma and goes directly to Oklahoma hospitals.

Is Our Blood Institute the same as OneBlood?

No. Our Blood Institute and OneBlood are completely separate, independent blood banking organizations. OBI is based in Oklahoma and serves the state's hospitals. OneBlood is based in Florida and serves Florida and the Southeast. They are not affiliated with each other.
